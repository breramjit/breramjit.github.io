about me

hello! my name is bre ramjit and i am a 1st year student at algonquin for the IMD program. i have just recently graduated from western university with a degree in criminology. i am interested in learning new skills from the IMD program so that i can hopefully pursue a career in graphic design and freelance photography.

in my free time i enjoy both baking sweets and cooking meals. i love trying new recipes whenever i can. further, i enjoy watercolour painting and making beaded jewelry, in fact i recently opened an Etsy shop where i sell both my paintings and jewelry. i also enjoy watching movies and my ideal idea of relaxing would be going to the movie theatre on my own or with my friends.
